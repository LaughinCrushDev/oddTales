DRAGOON (5e Subclass)


Masters of swift movement and longarm combat, these black knights have developed a tactical and precise aerial style of combat fit to pierce and destroy scaled and armored opponents of even the greatest of size.


Dragonfire Techniques

When you choose this archetype at 3rd level, you learn Techniques that are fueled by a special dice called Jump Dice.


Techniques. You learn three Techniques of your choice. Many of them will enhance your movement or help you position yourself and your allies in combat in some way. You can only use one technique per attack.


You learn two additional techniques of your choice at 7th, 10th, and 15th level. Each time you learn new techniques, you may replace one you already know with a different one as well.


Jump Dice. You have four jump dice, which are d8s. A superiority die is expended when you use it. You regain all your superiority dice after you finish a short or long rest. You gain another superiority dice at 10th level and one more at 15th level.


Saving Throws. Some techniques require your target to make a saving throw to resist the technique’s effects. The saving throw DC is calculated as follows:


Technique Save DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (your choice).


Sole Stomp

At 3rd level, a dragoon learns how to properly land on a target, or survive the landing after a failed dive. You take halved damage from any fall, and take no damage from falling on top of a space occupied by another creature.


If no other spell, feature or technique override this effect; you are then forced 5 feet in any direction of your choosing away from that creature.


If that creature’s AC is below your Technique Save DC, they receive 1 + your Fighter Level piercing damage.


Vorpal Thrust

At 7th level, anytime after you’ve landed from a fall or jump, you may make an attack with a weapon as a bonus action that turn.


High Jump

At 10th level, your Jump action, or any action that uses the effect of a Jump action, also moves you like if you had taken the Dash action.


Eye of the Dragon

At 15th level, your Jump Dice become d10s. At 18th level, they become d12s.
